# Hi there, welcome to Picinview.

A small corner on the internet — built with care, emotion, and a bit of **vibe coding**.

## 🎐 About This Project

This website is more than just a technical project.

Every part of it — the structure, the interactions, and especially the photos inside — represents something meaningful to me.  
Even during development, simply opening the page already made me happy.

It started as an implementation, but somewhere along the way, it became something personal.

## 💈 Why It Exists

I don’t expect this to be something huge.  
But I do hope:

- More people might come across this place someday  
- Maybe even find it through a simple Google search  
- And spend a quiet moment here

Think of it as a **little backyard on the internet**.  
You’re always welcome to stop by, look around, and just exist for a bit.

## 📸 What You'll Find

- Photos that matter to me  
- A simple, evolving web experience  
- A space that reflects both code and emotion
